# Shipment Document Checklist  

---

## 1. Commercial Documents

Commercial Invoice  
Packing List  
Sales Contract or Term Sheet Reference  

---

## 2. Transport Documents

Bill of Lading / Sea Waybill / Air Waybill  
Charter Party (if applicable)  
Delivery Order  

---

## 3. Insurance Documents

Cargo Insurance Policy or Certificate  
Insurance Coverage Details  

---

## 4. Origin and Compliance

Certificate of Origin  
Export License (if applicable)  
Import License (if applicable)  
Sanctions and Compliance Declaration  

---

## 5. Quality and Quantity

Certificate of Analysis  
Certificate of Quantity / Weight  
Inspection Certificate  

---

## 6. Customs and Regulatory

Export Customs Declaration  
Import Customs Declaration  
HS Code Confirmation  
Regulatory Certificates (commodity-specific)  

---

## 7. Financial and Banking

Payment Instruction Sheet  
Letter of Credit Documentation (if applicable)  
Bank Endorsements or Releases  

---

## 8. Environmental and Sustainability (if applicable)

Sustainability Certificate  
Carbon or Emissions Documentation  
Chain of Custody Evidence  

---

## 9. Final Delivery and Close-Out

Proof of Delivery  
Demurrage / Dispatch Statement  
Final Settlement Statement  

---

## 10. Record Retention

All shipment documents shall be retained in accordance with internal policy, bank requirements, and applicable regulatory standards.
